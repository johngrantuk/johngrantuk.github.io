This is me

I'm an engineer from Edinburgh, Scotland. Since 2007 I've worked in the satellite industry most recently as the Head Of Technology at Scot Sat, a designer of high-end, remote satellite communications equipment for the maritime industry.

I've got a wide range of experience and interests including software development, electronics, RF engineering and communications. Recently I've been enjoying experimenting with Ethereum and have been trying to contribute to the IOTA Python library.
